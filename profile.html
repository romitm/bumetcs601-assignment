Romit Maity

BU MET CS601 Web Application Development

Assignments - Spring 2015

Experience:

I am a Software Profesional with over 10 years of Full Time Work Experience as a Developer and Consultant. I started my career in the year 2004 working for a company named Cognizant Technology Solutions, US Corp. (Nasdaq: CTSH) where I joined as a Developer and went onto become a Senior Developer. I worked in the capacity of a Consultant Developer at a leading Airlines company based out of Eagan MN for three years. Then I moved to Framnigham MA and worked for a large Office Supplies Retailer in the capacity of a Consultant for the next seven and a half years.
Currently I am working at a leading eCommerce Cloud Based company Demandware, Inc. (Nasdaq: DWRE). I joined the company at its Burlington Mass. Headquarters in July of 2014. I am a 'Solutions Architect' at the company and I have the responsibility of building out the solution and strategy for any Retail shop to get onto the eCommerce bandwagon as seamlessly and as rapidly possible. I alos have the responsibility to partner with the COE (Center of Excellence) to provide Best Practices and layout the rubrics for global deployment. We are currently a global company with locations in Australia, Netherlands, Germany, Denmark, France, UK and the US.

Education:

I was awarded my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Electrical Engineering from Netaji Subhash Engineering College in India. I completed my Higher Secondary and Secondary schooling from Don Bosco School in India. I always had a penchant for Computer systems and the digital world. Immediatele after finishing college I joined a Software cmpany so that I could comprehend the Programming skills and validate the learnings at an industrial level. This allowed me to go further beyond the realms of textbooks and I was able to harness more of the concepts while being there on the field and working as a Corporate Consultant. After about a decade, I decided to join a Graduate program to complete the full circle of furthering my education and interweave the concepts that I have applied till date to the modern technologies and ever changing landscape of Information Technology.

Interests:

Currently residing in Central Massachusetts with my family I spend a lot of time with my family when I am not working. I love to plan for short weekend getaways with my wife and I have been to 43 of the Lower 48 states. I am an amateur photographer and practice Lansdscape and Night Photography with teams of people in the Greater Boston area. I love to shoot the Night sky and Stars on a very clear night. I have been to a number of the lighthouses in New England and New York area to photograph at night. Eventually if I am lucky and have the resources and time, I might take up my passion as my full time profession.
