Video & Motion Picture

  • Video will be frames captured by a recording device which will show smooth transition and motion.
  • Number of FPS should be 16 or higher to relay motion. PAL has 24 FPS and NTSC has 29.97 FPS.
  • With the onset of digital technology it has easier to shoot Video and render on any playback device which can decode digital information.
  • Color spaces, Video quality and Video compression are factors in rendering a quality video for the consumers to enjoy.
  • Video files can be stored on Analog Tape formats, Digital Tape formats, Optical Disc Storage formats.
