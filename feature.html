Time Lapse Photography: What it is and what it is not.

  • It is not a Motion Picture! For a Motion Piture the frame rate has to be 24 fps or higher. It is captured with an exposure and a time interval factored in.
  • These are pictures captured, let's say, once every five seconds but played at 30 fps (frames per second) and this would give it a smooth motion.
  • This technique has evolved to capture the Nightscape, Crowds, Cityscapes, and even capture the development in the growth patterns of flora and fauna.
  • Currently Time Lapse has become a niche way to portray an artists appreciation for the environment and surroundings.
  • Now people are combining various motion picture strategies like pan, tilt and zoom to make their Time Lapse unique, creative and evocative.
